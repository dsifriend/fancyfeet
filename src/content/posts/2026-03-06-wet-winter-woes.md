Hey! Did you think I was done with this? Sorry about that. A couple things happened that kept me from writing an update, but here we are!


Let’s start with the main one, which is that the weather’s been really weird this year. Most of the attention that was given to this was directed at the week long snow storm that hit the Northeast, and rightfully so, but that doesn’t mean we weren’t affected down here in Puerto Rico.


It’s been unusually cool all January and February, and it was *nonstop rain* the week of the storm. Because of that we didn’t see most of the cats in all that time, since well, cats like wet about as much as I like paying rent. Not sure where they were hiding to be perfectly honest with you, but really only Creamsicle showed his face.


![An orange-white cat sitting pretty among some tree roots](/content/media/IMG_4523.jpeg "Creamsicle, aka Praline")

Oh, that’s another thing: most of the neighbors have taken to calling him Praline instead. I believe there’s much less consensus on everybody else’s names however.
