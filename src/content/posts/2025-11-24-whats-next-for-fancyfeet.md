I did mention this in the first post, about Menina, but I didn't want to go into details,
since I felt it might retract from my message, so here's a full overview changes
coming to [FancyFeet.Cat](https://fancyfeet.cat/):

1. This is no longer a joke content aggregation blog.
2. That doesn't mean I'll be 100% serious 100% of the time.
3. There's some technical upgrades. Try subscribing in your RSS reader!

I want to make this a place on the Internet for cat-lovers to express themselves
and share in the love they have with these curious furry-feeted companions
to the human race. Me personally, I'll be volunteering at a local cat welfare
organization soon, and separate from that trying to figure out what's going on
with the local cat colony in my neighborhood. I'll be documenting both.
